Worthing Street Game

An interactive adventure through the seaside town of Worthing. Complete challenges, discover landmarks, and uncover local history.

Project by Nancy Carter, 2025

Discover Worthing's Hidden Stories

This project's objective is to create an enjoyable, interactive street game, playable on any mobile device over a small, accessible, mapped area in central Worthing.

Explore the Map

Navigate through an interactive map of Worthing. Discover key landmarks and hidden locations as you explore.

Complete Challenges

Test your knowledge and observation skills with location-based challenges. Earn achievements as you progress.

Discover History

Learn about Worthing's rich history and culture. Uncover stories and facts about the town's significant landmarks.

Key Lessons Learned

"The nebulous nature of a project without a budget and run by a single person is challenging. The key learning point is to start much smaller, with well-defined limitations."

Refined Approach:

  • Create a simple game prototype to generate interest.
  • Focus on three working clue journeys with yes/no/hint trees.
  • Include one example of a mocked-up sponsor opportunity.
  • Specifically exclude branding, marketing, and full development from the initial scope.
